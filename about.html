About Us

Your long-term manufacturing partner.

Precision Manufacturing Co. is an ISO-certified machining and fabrication partner for OEMs and industrial customers who need reliable components, consistent quality, and on-time delivery.

We started with a single manual lathe and a small welding bay. Today, we operate a modern facility with CNC turning, milling, fabrication and assembly capabilities – but our core principle hasn’t changed: build parts like they matter in real-world conditions.

Our team combines experienced machinists, welding experts, and quality engineers who understand that a “simple” bracket or shaft is often critical to uptime and safety in the field.

Key facts

  • 20+ years in machining and fabrication
  • ISO 9001:2015-certified quality management
  • Serving automotive, material handling, construction and energy
  • Mix of low-volume development and high-volume production
How we work

Process-driven, but pragmatic.

Our processes are structured enough for consistency, yet flexible enough to handle new developments and engineering changes without chaos.

Engineering-first mindset

We don’t just “take orders.” We review drawings, ask questions, and highlight risks in manufacturability, material selection, and tolerance stack-ups.

Transparent communication

You get realistic lead times, clear updates, and early warnings – not surprises at the last minute.

Continuous improvement

We regularly update fixtures, tooling, and process parameters to reduce cycle times and improve repeatability without compromising quality.

Journey

From a small workshop to a full facility.

  • 2005 – Founded with a single manual lathe and a basic welding bay.
  • 2010 – Added first CNC turning centers to support automotive and material handling OEMs.
  • 2015 – Expanded into fabrication, frames, and machine bases with dedicated welding bays.
  • 2020 – Upgraded quality lab and implemented ISO 9001:2015-certified QMS.
  • Today – Focused on being a reliable, long-term partner for customers who value consistency over shortcuts.
